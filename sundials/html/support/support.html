  3.subscribe and post to the mailing list.


FAQ and Usage Notes

Answers to some of the most common user questions, as well as several tips on installing and using SUNDIALS can be found in the Frequently Asked Questions and Usage Notes section.

Some lengthier discussions of various issues are available on the Sundials Wiki (external link). Topics currently covered include: integration return and output times, controlling unphysical solutions and integrating over discontinuities, and switching on/off forward sensitivity computations.
